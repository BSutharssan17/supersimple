NLE Choppa

NLE Choppa(real name Bryson LaShaun Potts) is a 22 year old(born on November 1st 2002 in Memphis). His First track was Nolove Anthem which was released in 2018. His first track that propelled him to Billboard was his Shotta Flow series which he remixed some of the songs with Chief Keef, Blueface and Lil Mabu

NLE Choppa's albums

he has released several albums including Narco Choppa(2019), Holly Trap(2019) with NoCap, Cottonwood, Top Shotta, CottonWood 2, From dark to Light, Shotta Flow series and others

NLE Choppa's latest album

NLE Choppa's latest 2 albums are S*** SZN and Picasso: SLUFFIN SZN x S*** SZN where he has collaborated with the likes of Summer walker, Yung Bleu, Skilla Baby, T-Pain, 41(Kyle Richh, Jenn Carter, TaTa) and others
