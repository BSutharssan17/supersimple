Polo G

Polo G(real name is Taurus Tremani Barlett) was born on 6 January 1999(age is 26 years old) is a Chicago rapper. His first song was "Gang" which he released in 2015 but his first track to hit the billboard tracks was "Pop Out" featuring Bronx rapper Lil Tjay

He has collaborated with the likes of: The memphis rappers Moneybagg Yo, NLE Choppa, GloRilla, Bronx rappers Pop Smoke, Flvio Foreign, Lil Tjay and other rappers such as: Roddy Ricch, Nicki Minaj, Young Thug, G Herbo, The Kid LAROI, Lil Durk etc

Polo's 3 most successful albums(Die for Legends, Hall of Fame and Hall of Fame 2.0)

Polo G's first album was called Hall of Fame and it gained 143,000 copies in its first week making it the first album Polo G to reach billboard status and his success grew with his next album the GOAT and a deluxe version where the 3 including Hall of Fame and GOAT album were combined to give the BEST album.

Polo's latest album

Polo G's latest album was called HOOD POET(He overcame obstacles during Pain or Emotional Trauma) which include features from Chicago rappers G Herbo and Lil Durk, Memphis Rapper GloRilla and The Kid LAROI and Future and others involved in the album
