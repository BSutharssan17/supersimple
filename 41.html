41 (Kyle Richh, Jenn Carter, TaTa)

41 is a group based in Brownsville, East Brooklyn known for a combination of street and music related to money and women.

The group is based on Kyle Richh (22, real name Henry Olatunde Fasheun), Jenn Carter (22, real name Jennifer Oghenekevwe Akpofure) and TaTa (20, real name Zaire Tysean Rivera).

Kyle Richh and Jenn Carter knew each other through their parents and grew up seeing each other often in the neighbourhood. The trio were formed in the COVID-19 Pandemic when both Kyle Richh and Jenn Carter were introduced to TaTa through their mutual friendship with fellow rapper Dee Billz.

They are known for tracks "Bent" (remix with Sexyy Red), "Notti Bop", "Run That" and many others. They have performed at Rolling Loud Miami and many other tours around America and the world.
